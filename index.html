MemoryCompanion

A published research project that builds a cutting-edge digital health tool designed to enhance care for Alzheimer's disease (AD) patients using generative AI, specifically through GPT technology.

The system personalizes interactions using voice cloning and talking-face mechanisms, making communication feel more familiar and human-like for patients. This aims to alleviate social isolation and improve cognitive function and daily communication for AD patients.

MemoryCompanion is built into 4 modules:

  1. Real Time Speech-to-Text Module: enables real-time vocal communication between AD patients and the GPT model
  2. Patient-Centric MemoryCompanion Model Fine-Tuning: processes transcribed input into a tailored AD-Patient-GPT language model for personalized conversation
  3. Text-to-Speech Synthesization: voices the GPT output
  4. Face Construction: emotion-sensitive talking-face interface for authentic interaction

This research was published at ML4H 2023 in New Orleans, and presented at QUWA (2023) and NYUAD AI Workshop (2024).

Political Preference Prediction Model

A study that explores the possibility of predicting political preferences based on Twitter content during the 2020 U.S. election cycle.

Using Natural Language Processing (NLP), tweets were analyzed for sentiment and topic frequency to correlate with political affiliations.

  1. Collect Tweets: Dataset from Kaggle with 1.72M tweets tagged #JoeBiden and #DonaldTrump
  2. Identify Political Topics: Based on independent, nonpartisan sources
  3. Topic Tracking: Using spaCy to detect topic frequency and build feature vectors
  4. Sentiment Analysis: Using VADER to score each tweet

Models used: SVC, Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, KNN. Results show the potential of social media data in political forecasting.

Proximity

A GPS-based business model platform to connect student-run businesses with nearby customers.

Watch explainer video

This startup idea was developed after observing students' difficulty in finding enough clients. We conducted deep market research, built user hypotheses, and analyzed strategic forces in the higher education ecosystem.

The platform empowers student entrepreneurs and offers fast, affordable access to services and goods. Starting at NYU Abu Dhabi, Proximity aims to scale via a university ambassador program.
